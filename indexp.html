My Work as an UX/UI/Graphic Designer

I had the oportunity to work as a web design project manager for six months at ZOEK Marketing. while i was there, i was able to make amazing things with the ux and programming team. Mi job was to supervise every project, talking with every client to understand their needs and motivations for it's business.
 , Once i understood their needs, i was able to transmit the idea with the UX and design team, after that we had to prototype it's website on figma, sketch and dreamweaver.
Sometimes, clients didn't want code on their webpages and, so the ux team was able to layout directly on WIX, Webflow or Wordpress (mostly WIX bc of ZOEK and Wix Partnership), and in other ocations we had to give the prototypes to the programming team.
I was supervising the project, so i wasn't to much on programming there, but it was my job to check everything was fine.

On the other hand, i have my own entrepreneurship project called Webcrafters. I'm the main UX designer and project manager where i usually direct design, motion graphics, animation and programming teams. Also we work externally with photography, branding and marketing teams, so we can offer a more complete service. At this day we're only an online web design and marketing agency, we have worked with people around the world thanks to Fiverr and UpWork, where we offer our services.

 My job was to redesign (with another 4 talented designers) it's main website. It was a 3 month's project. I designed with Figma, Photoshop, Ilustrator, Animated with Jitter, Maqueted with Dreamweaver and programmed with Webflow. It was Amazing, i love the results.
